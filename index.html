About Me

Hi, I'm Heather.

I'm a BACP Accredited Counsellor and Psychotherapist based in Leeds, with over eight years of experience supporting individuals through some of life's most challenging moments.

My practice draws on Person-Centred, Cognitive Behavioural (CBT) and EMDR approaches, tailored to what works best for you. I work with adults facing anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship difficulties, grief and more.

Sessions are available in person at my practice in Headingley, or online via secure video call.

My Approach

How we work together

There's no single path to feeling better. I draw on a range of evidence-based approaches and adapt them to suit what's right for you.

Person-Centred

A warm, non-directive space where you lead. I offer genuine empathy, unconditional positive regard and congruence — the conditions for natural growth and healing.

CBT

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy helps identify and gently challenge unhelpful thought patterns and behaviours, building practical skills you can use every day.

EMDR

Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing is a clinically proven therapy for trauma and PTSD, helping the mind process and integrate difficult memories.

How It Works

Getting started is simple

Taking the first step can feel daunting. I've made the process as straightforward as possible.

01

Reach Out

Fill in the contact form or send me an email. I'll get back to you within 48 hours to arrange a free 15-minute phone consultation.

02

First Session

Your first session is an opportunity to meet, discuss what brings you to therapy, and decide together whether we're a good fit — no pressure.

03

Ongoing Support

We meet weekly or fortnightly, working at a pace that suits you. Sessions are 50 minutes and held at the same time each week for consistency.
